# Sample Onboarding Copy

## Screen 1

**Build control without pressure**

TEMPO gives you a structured plan for awareness, pauses, recovery, movement, and healthier routines.

## Screen 2

**Private by design**

Your answers and session history stay on this iPhone. No account and no internet are required.

## Screen 3

**Not a diagnosis**

TEMPO can guide practice, but pain, urinary symptoms, unusual discharge, blood, or sudden major changes need professional assessment.

## Screen 4

**Rest is part of training**

The app may recommend breathing, walking, or recovery instead of another session.
