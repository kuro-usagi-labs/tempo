# Sample Health and Safety Copy

## Irritation

**Give your body time to recover**

You reported soreness or irritation. Guided sessions are paused for now. Avoid friction until the area feels normal again.

## Urinary or discharge symptoms

**A health check is the better next step**

Burning during urination or unusual discharge can have causes that a training app cannot assess. Pause sexual training and contact a qualified healthcare professional.

## Persistent acquired change

**Consider a professional assessment**

You said this change is new and continues to bother you. A clinician can check for contributing medical or psychological factors that the app cannot diagnose.

## Emergency-style symptoms

**Seek urgent medical care**

Severe pain, major swelling or injury, heavy bleeding, fainting, or rapidly worsening symptoms require urgent care rather than another app session.
