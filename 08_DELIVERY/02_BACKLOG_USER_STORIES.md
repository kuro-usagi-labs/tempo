# Backlog and User Stories

## Epic: Private onboarding

- As a user, I can confirm I am an adult.
- As a user, I can choose discreet terminology.
- As a user, I can enable Face ID before entering sensitive information.
- As a user, I can understand that data stays on my phone.

## Epic: Automatic plan

- As a user, I receive a useful schedule without designing it myself.
- As a user, missed activities do not become an overwhelming backlog.
- As a user, I can see why a task was chosen.

## Epic: Immediate urge support

- As a user, I can tap one obvious button when aroused.
- As a user, I receive a recommendation based on intensity, context, history, and safety.
- As a user, I can calm an urge without being pushed into training.

## Epic: Guided training

- As a user, I receive a clear stop warning before it is too late.
- As a user, I can use haptics without watching the screen.
- As a user, I can finish early without being labeled a failure.
- As a user, the next session adapts to my history.

## Epic: Healthy routine

- As a beginner, I receive manageable walking and strength tasks.
- As a user, rest counts as progress.
- As a user, exercise adapts after soreness or missed tasks.

## Epic: Safety

- As a user with warning symptoms, I am not given more sexual training.
- As a user, I can understand when self-training is insufficient.

## Epic: Privacy

- As a user, my lock screen never shows explicit text.
- As a user, I can delete everything in one action.
- As a user, I can verify that the app works offline.
