# Product Copy and Terminology

## Direct mode labels

- I’m aroused now
- Guided control session
- Arousal level
- Pause before it is too late
- Private session
- Sexual-health check

## Discreet mode labels

- Quick check-in
- Guided session
- Intensity level
- Pause now
- Personal session
- Health check

## Good copy examples

> Your arousal rose quickly last time. Today, pause one level earlier.

> Rest is part of the program. Another session today is unlikely to help.

> Pre-ejaculate is a normal response and does not mean the session failed.

> You reported irritation. Guided training is paused while your body recovers.

> Three controlled pauses matter more than a longer timer.

## Avoid

- “You failed.”
- “Your masculinity score.”
- “Beat your record.”
- “Cure premature ejaculation.”
- “Detox your brain.”
- “Never masturbate again.”
- “Your dopamine is damaged.”

## Warning copy

### Threshold warning

> **Pause now**  
> Hands off. Let the intensity fall.

### Safety warning

> **Stop training for now**  
> Your answers suggest this needs a health assessment rather than another session.

### Early completion

> **Session complete**  
> This is useful data. Next time, TEMPO will prompt you to pause earlier.
