# Open Product Decisions

These choices should be decided before visual production:

1. Final app name and bundle display name.
2. Direct Indonesian copy versus bilingual launch.
3. Minimum iOS version.
4. Whether HealthKit is included in MVP or 1.1.
5. Whether private-session logging is on or off by default.
6. Default guided-session cap: 20 or 25 minutes.
7. Default spacing between guided sessions: 48 or 72 hours.
8. Whether the user can override a rest recommendation immediately or after a short explanation.
9. Whether notes are included at all in MVP.
10. Whether the sideload build includes an Apple Watch target.
11. Clinician review process and named medical owner.
12. Intended distribution: private sideload only, TestFlight, or eventual App Store.

Recommended defaults:

- Indonesian first;
- iOS 17 baseline;
- HealthKit in 1.1;
- private-session logging off by default;
- 20-minute default cap;
- 48-hour default spacing;
- no notes in MVP;
- iPhone-only MVP;
- private sideload before TestFlight.
