# Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| App increases performance anxiety | High | Hide duration, emphasize control, adaptive recovery |
| Users train too frequently | High | Weekly cap, rest scoring, overtraining rules |
| Medical condition mistaken for habit issue | High | Safety gate, acquired-change prompts, clinician review |
| Sensitive data exposure | High | Local-only, biometric lock, privacy cover, no trackers |
| Red warning feels alarming | Medium | Brief, purposeful motion; calm recovery transition |
| HealthKit permission reduces trust | Medium | Optional, delayed request, clear explanation |
| Exercise causes injury | Medium | beginner progression, contraindication questions, replace task |
| App becomes a pornography companion | High | no erotic content, no media import, no stimulation content |
| User chases scores | Medium | no leaderboard, duration secondary, rest rewarded |
| Sideload signing expires or fails | Medium | clear build/signing guide, paid developer option |
| Rule engine becomes hard to maintain | Medium | typed rules, reason codes, versioning, exhaustive tests |
| App Store review concerns | Medium | health-focused copy, 18+, no explicit media, clinician review |
