# Content Governance

## Review process

Every health lesson and safety rule should be reviewed by:

- a qualified clinician in urology, andrology, sexual medicine, or related practice;
- a product safety reviewer;
- a plain-language editor.

## Content metadata

Each module stores:

- content version;
- reviewer role;
- review date;
- source references;
- next review date;
- applicable locales.

## Update policy for offline builds

Because the app is offline, medical content changes ship with app updates. Display the content version in Settings.

## Claims policy

Allowed:

- “supports awareness”;
- “guides structured practice”;
- “may help some users build control”;
- “supports exercise consistency.”

Not allowed:

- “clinically proven cure” without matching evidence and regulatory review;
- “guaranteed to last longer”;
- “repairs dopamine”;
- “treats every cause.”

## Tone policy

No moral judgment about masturbation or pornography. Discuss patterns, control, distress, and life impact rather than purity or shame.
