# Weekly Schedule Templates

## Beginner template

| Day | Primary task | Secondary task |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Breathing 4 min | Walk 15–20 min |
| Tuesday | Guided awareness session | Reflection 1 min |
| Wednesday | Recovery | Mobility 6 min |
| Thursday | Cardio 20 min | Education module |
| Friday | Guided control session | Calm breathing |
| Saturday | Beginner strength 15 min | Optional walk |
| Sunday | Weekly review | Schedule recalculation |

## Standard template

| Day | Primary task | Secondary task |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Cardio 25 min | Relaxation 5 min |
| Tuesday | Guided control session | Reflection |
| Wednesday | Strength 20 min | Mobility |
| Thursday | Recovery | Education |
| Friday | Guided control session | Breath reset |
| Saturday | Cardio 25–35 min | Optional strength |
| Sunday | Weekly review | Next-week plan |

## High-stress template

- Reduce guided training to one session.
- Prioritize sleep, walking, breathing, and recovery.
- Never increase difficulty when anxiety is 8/10 or higher.
- Prompt user to reassess after 48 hours.

## Irritation template

- Suspend sexual training for 48–72 hours.
- Offer gentle movement and education.
- Re-screen symptoms before resuming.
- Escalate to health guidance if pain persists.

## Scheduling constraints

- Never place guided sessions on consecutive days by default.
- Never schedule more than three guided sessions in seven days.
- Keep at least one full recovery day.
- Do not send explicit notification language.
- Respect user-defined quiet hours.
- Recalculate after missed tasks without stacking multiple tasks into one day.
