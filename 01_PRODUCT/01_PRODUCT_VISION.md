# Product Vision

## Vision

Create the most private and structured iPhone companion for adults who want to understand their arousal, stop rushing, and build healthier sexual and physical routines without accounts, judgment, pornography, or AI.

## Positioning

TEMPO is not presented as a masturbation tracker. It is positioned as a **private control and wellness training app**.

### Category

- Primary: Health & Fitness / sexual wellness
- Secondary: habit coaching, mindfulness, self-guided training

### One-line pitch

> A private offline coach that tells you what to do today and guides you through each pause.

## Product principles

### 1. Private by default

No account, no cloud, no ads, no trackers, and no explicit notification text.

### 2. Structured, not obsessive

The app controls training frequency and discourages repetitive “testing.”

### 3. Calm, not shame-based

The language never labels the user dirty, broken, weak, addicted, or less masculine.

### 4. Adaptive, not mysterious

Every recommendation can show a simple reason:

> “Recovery day recommended because you trained yesterday and reported irritation.”

### 5. Useful without internet

All rules, articles, schedules, sounds, haptics, and calculations ship inside the app.

### 6. Independence is success

The final phase reduces prompts and teaches the user to operate without a timer.

## North-star behavior

A user opens TEMPO during an urge, completes a 15-second check-in, receives a safe recommendation, and follows it without needing to decide what program to invent.
